▲ Copy Presentation: To Thumb Drive and Eject

Description: Copy the frontmost presentation to the mounted thumb drive and eject after the copy has completed.
Scope: Keynote
Command Variations: To use this command, speak any of the following phrases. Words within brackets [ ] are optional and my be omitted if desired.
  • save [this] presentation to [my] thumb drive and eject [it]
  • copy [this] presentation to [my] thumb drive and eject [it]
