▲ Play from the Beginning

Description: Play the frontmost document from the beginning
Scope: QuickTime Player
Command Variations: To use this command, speak any of the following phrases. Words within brackets [ ] are optional and my be omitted if desired.
  • play [this] [that] back
  • play [this] [that] from [the] beginning
  • play [this] [that] from [the] top
  • play [this] [that] from [the] start
