▲ Template Assist: School Poster Small

Description: This command will open a new copy of the School Poster Small template and interactively prompt you to enter text and choose image files to replace the placeholders in the new document.
Scope: Pages
Command Variations: To use this command, speak any of the following phrases. Words within brackets [ ] are optional and my be omitted if desired.
  • assist [me] with [the] small school poster template
  • assist [me] with [the] template school poster small
  • assist [me] with [the] template small school poster
