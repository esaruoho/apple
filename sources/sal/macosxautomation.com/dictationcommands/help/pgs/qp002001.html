▲ Close without Saving

Description: Close the frontmost document without saving
Scope: QuickTime Player
Command Variations: To use this command, speak any of the following phrases. Words within brackets [ ] are optional and my be omitted if desired.
  • close without saving
  • close [this] movie without saving
  • close [this] clip without saving
  • close [this] recording without saving
  • close [the] front movie without saving
  • close [the] front clip without saving
  • close [the] front recording without saving
  • close [this] movie and don't save it
  • close [this] clip and don't save it
  • close [this] recording and don't save it
  • close [the] front movie and don't save it
  • close [the] front clip and don't save it
  • close [the] front recording and don't save it
