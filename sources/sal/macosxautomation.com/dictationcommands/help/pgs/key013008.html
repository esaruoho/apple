▲ Speak the body of current slide

Description: Speak the content of the default body text of the current slide.
Scope: Keynote
Command Variations: To use this command, speak any of the following phrases. Words within brackets [ ] are optional and my be omitted if desired.
  • read [the] [slide] body
  • speak [the] [slide] body
  • read [the] body of this slide
  • speak [the] body of this slide
