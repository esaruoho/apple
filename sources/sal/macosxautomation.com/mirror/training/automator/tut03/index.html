Tutorial 03: Daily NASA Desktop Image

Here's a workflow that will keep life intersting by providing a daily surprise when you first sit infront of your computer.

This workflow will download an image from the NASA Image of the Day feed and make it the current desktop picture. In addition, this workflow will be saved as an iCal alarm so it will update your desktop automatically every day.
