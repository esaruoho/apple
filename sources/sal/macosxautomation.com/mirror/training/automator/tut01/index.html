Tutorial 01: Personalized Mail Messages

Here’s a great way to personalize your outgoing email messages—use Mail’s stationery feature and add your image to the message!

It’s simple and easy to create an Automator workflow that will deliver the candid shot that gives your messages that heart-felt feeling. All you need is:

  1. A laptop or desktop Mac with a built-in iSight camera,
  2. and Automator
