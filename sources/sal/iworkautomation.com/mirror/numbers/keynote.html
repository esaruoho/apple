Exporting to Keynote

To a Numbers master, Keynote is the bridge from the numerically intuitive to the rationally inspirational. Data can be dangerous, for it’s always a distinct possibility that a single table or chart can change the direction of a project or a company. So to keep the mind’s eye upon the cauldron of calculation, those with a mission use automation to expunge the drudgery, delivering the facts fast, with vision true and reason intact.

In other words… this topic is about using AppleScript to transform data between Numbers and Keynote, from tables on a spreadsheet to charts on slide.

Abracadabra!
