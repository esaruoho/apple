**17 sessions across 13 years.** Sal at Apple 1997-2016. WWDC 2016 had no Sal session — he was already out by the time the conference happened. The 2018 #717 "Workflow → Shortcuts" session was Apple announcing the technology that replaced what Sal got fired for.

## The arc, in one paragraph

2003 is the AppleScript Studio explosion — peer-to-Aqua framing, four sessions in one week. 2004 introduces Automator (Tiger). 2005 broadens the pitch to compiled-language devs. 2007 doubles up on Automator with Leopard. 2008-2009 deepens Automator + Services. 2010-2011 stretches to iPad content + AppleScript-ObjC. 2012 is the bridge talk where automation survives sandboxing. 2013 doubles up: AppleScript Libraries + Mavericks update. 2014 ships JXA. 2015 is enterprise-focused, Sal's swansong. Then silence.
