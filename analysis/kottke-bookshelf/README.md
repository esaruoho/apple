# Daniel Kottke's "Steve Jobs Memorial Bookshelf"

**Source:** Ellen Petry Leanse, Medium (May 1, 2015). Kottke presented the list at the Dent Conference, March 2015.

**Context:** Daniel Kottke — Apple employee #12, Jobs's closest friend at Reed College (1972), travel companion to India (1974), Apple-1 board assembler. These are the books he and Jobs shared.
