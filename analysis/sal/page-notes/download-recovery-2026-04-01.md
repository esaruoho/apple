## Notes

- The indexer at `bin/sal-index-download-targets.py` ignores zero-byte files so failed downloads cannot produce false positives.
- The downloader at `bin/sal-recover-downloads.py` tries the live artifact first, then falls back to Wayback CDX results for the same URL when needed.
- Recovered media files are retained locally for later viewing/transcription and are not committed because `sources/sal/**/*.mp4`, `*.m4v`, and `*.mov` are ignored.
- Oversized recovered bundles such as `AUTOWKSHP-03-Leader.zip` and `EPUB-Demo-Kit-Lg.zip` are retained locally and not committed because they exceed GitHub's 100 MB file limit.
- `dictationcommands.com` still has no native recovered bundles; its useful cross-linked recovery path is `macosxautomation.com/yosemite/JXA-example.zip`.
